Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to LetsJobify ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use LetsJobify, we collect the following information:

  • Job Preferences: Your job location preferences, sector, skills, and minimum salary expectations
  • Payment Information: Payment transaction details processed through Razorpay (we do not store your full payment card details)
  • Contact Information: If you contact us, we may collect your name, email address, and any other information you provide

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information and operating system
  • Usage data and interaction patterns
  • Log files and error reports

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our job search services
  • To process payments and fulfill your orders
  • To match job listings with your preferences using AI technology
  • To communicate with you about your account and our services
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Payment transactions are processed securely through Razorpay
  • Transaction records are stored in our secure database
  • Generated Excel files are stored temporarily and can be accessed via secure download links
  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect against unauthorized access

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Razorpay: Payment processing (see Razorpay's privacy policy)
  • Google Gemini AI: For enhancing job descriptions and matching (see Google's privacy policy)
  • RemoteOK: Job listings source (see RemoteOK's terms and privacy policy)

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Transaction records are retained for accounting and legal purposes
  • Generated Excel files are stored temporarily and may be deleted after a reasonable period
  • Job preferences are stored temporarily in memory during the order process

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Object to processing of your information
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at mishra.ritwik.03@gmail.com

8. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
